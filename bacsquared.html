About The Conference
The Business Analytics Club's Business Analytics Conference is BAC's signature event to further develop students’ understanding of analytics work and the underlying industry attached to it. In 2019, the second iteration of this conference attracted nearly 100 attendees and leading companies who the analytics world such as PwC, Spotify, IBM, Twitter, and AppNexus. The BAC^2 event consists of a Keynote presentation, a meet & greet with the companies, a corporate panel where representatives from companies share their insights and experience, followed by a networking reception with refreshments. We look forward to seeing all you business analytics gurus there at BAC^2 2020 in the spring!
