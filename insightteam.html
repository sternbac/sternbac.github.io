About The Insight Team
The Insight Team was launched in 2017 by the undergraduate Business Analytics Club. The primary goal is to build upon the technical skills of students through workshops and participation in a semester-long data science project. The Advanced Insight Team was introduced in Spring 2020 to offer students hands-on experience with real-world data and exposure to data analytics in the workforce. We hope these programs will foster a sense of community for students interested in the fields of tech and analytics as well as provide industry insight so that they can learn about career opportunities with firms that do not traditionally recruit at Stern.
