About BAC's Membership Tier System
This program tracks the attendance of BAC members and provides benefits to those most engaged with the club. The two types of BAC Members are General Members - Students who have attended at least one BAC weekly meeting and Prime Members - Students who have attended 5 meetings by the first cut-off date and 7 meetings by the second cut-off dates. Maintain your Prime status by continuing to attended BAC weekly meetings, making sure you attend at least half by each cutoff date.
